Nice n' Spooky

A DJ Sarah Playlist

Do you celebrate Halloween all year round? Are you a creature of the night? Have we got the playlist for you!

Song Country Genre Rating Comments

Icarus

Jason Webley		 United States Folk Punk 10/10 If you're a fan of the accordion, you will dig this jam about flying too close to the sun and drowning in the ocean. It's more fun than it sounds, I promise.

Saturnz Barz

Gorillaz		 England Electronica, Hip Hop 8/10 You can't go wrong with Gorillaz. If you happen to find yourself inside of a haunted house, play this song. You'll get on the ghost's good side.

The Mariner's Revenge Song

The Decemberists		 United States Alternative, Indie Folk 7/10 Here we have a sea shanty about being stuck inside of a whale with your worst enemy. It's the perfect time for revenge. While it is an absolute banger, it could be creepier.

Cast No Shadow

Brown Bird		 United States Folk 6/10 This one's a good one to play if you're in an apocalypse mood. Is your whole world on fire? We've got a song for that!

Secret

The Pierces		 United States Pop 9/10 The moral of this song is, basically, never tell anyone anything. Words to live by!

The Zombie

C.W. Stoneking		 Australia Blues 9/10 Who doesn't love zombies? This is a classic jam for when you want to shuffle around your room at 3am.

The Hangman's Song

Puerto Muerto		 United States Indie Alternative 8/10 This mellow tune, with it's Spanish-style acoustic guitar and harmonizing vocals, is so mesmerizing that you almost forget about how macabre it is!

Song for A Siren

The Jane Austen Argument		 Australia Cabaret, Indie Folk 9/10 We have another nautical themed song. Sure, houses can be haunted, and that's pretty cool, but a haunted ship? Now that's awesome. Ghost pirates: Two halloween costumes for the price of one.

Red Right Hand

Nick Cave & The Bad Seeds		 Australia Alternative, Gothic Rock 10/10 Very good and very creepy. This is the song you want to play when you're lurking in the shadows. You'll seem cool, like a vampire, instead of just a weird guy in a poorly lit corner.
